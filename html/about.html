Novara is a self-study platform built around active recall and reinforcement learning. You read, you get tested, you see what you are doing well with, and what needs work.

It tracks what you get wrong, surfaces those questions more often, and lets you run survival and practice sessions on top of your enrolled curriculums. Gamified only slightly, so its more about learning than about points and badges. The goal is to make it as easy as possible for students to learn more effectively, and to help them build better study habits along the way.

I built this website solo, as a method of learning coding, and also a way to help others learn more effectively. I hope it can be a useful tool for students around the world, and I'm excited to keep improving it over time.
