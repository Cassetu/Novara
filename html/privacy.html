PRIVACY POLICY

FROM NOVARA

What Data We Collect

Novara collects your email address, name, learning progress and scores.

Why We Collect Data

To save your learning progress across devices, and prevent spam bots. Also used to store your data/personalization. I designed Novara to be passwordless.

How We Store It

Using Firebase/Firestore, an infrastructure from Google designed for authentication.

Who Has Access

Only Me, The Developer. Your data is not sold or shared with third parties.

How Long We Keep Your Data

Until you choose to delete it.

How To Delete Your Data

Contact me at cassetunium@gmail.com or use the purge button under settings to immediately clear it!
